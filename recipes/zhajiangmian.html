Zhajiangmian

A popular classic Beijing dish, Zha Jiang Mian features ground pork and onion cooked in a rich savory brown sauce with a hint of sweetness, tossed with noodles and then served with crunchy cucumber. It takes no time to prepare, is easy to cook, and can be prepared ahead of time. Itâ€™s a perfect one-bowl dish for hot days and is great for meal prep as well.

Ingredients

  • Sweet bean paste and water
  • Sliced cucumber (and other toppings if you prefer)
  • Minced ginger
  • Shaoxing wine
  • Oil for cooking
  • Ground pork
  • Chopped onion

Instructions

  1. Gently cook the ginger
  2. Pan fry the pork
  3. Add the sauce
  4. Simmer while stirring to bring the flavor out of the sauce
  5. Add the onions and cook until tender
  6. Cook the noodles and mix them into the sauce
  7. Top with cucumber and other toppings
